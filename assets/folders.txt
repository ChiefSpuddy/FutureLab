You'll need to create an "assets" folder and add the following images:

1. hero-image.png - An illustration of web/app development
2. cardwizz-mockup.jpg - A screenshot or mockup of your CardWizz app
3. pokespud-mockup.jpg - A screenshot of your PokeSpud website
4. profile-image.jpg - A professional headshot or profile photo of yourself

These can be obtained from:
- Stock photos websites like Unsplash or Pexels
- Screenshots of your existing work
- Generated using design tools or AI image generators
